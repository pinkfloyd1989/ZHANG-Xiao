Research Interests

  • Online Learning & Reinforcement Learning: Theoretical analysis and algorithm design for online learning & reinforcement learning, low-resource stream data learning algorithms.
  • Causal Learning & Trustworthy AI: Causal bias correction, counterfactual learning algorithms, controllable AI, and fair machine learning research.
  • Information Retrieval Applications: Applications in recommendation systems, search ranking, with a focus on leveraging large models for information retrieval.

Biography

Xiao Zhang is an Associate Professor at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence at Renmin University of China, a doctoral advisor, and a distinguished young scholar. He has published over 40 papers in top academic conferences and journals such as ICML, NeurIPS, KDD, SIGIR, AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, ICDE, and VLDB. His work has received several prestigious nominations and awards, including Best Short Paper Nominee at SIGIR 2024, Best Paper Nominee at VLDB 2024, and Best Paper Award at SIGIR-AP 2023. His online kernel learning algorithms were recognized as "promising for large-scale matrix computation and online learning" by the IAPR Newsletter. He serves as a committee member for conferences like ICML, KDD, SIGIR, NeurIPS, and AAAI. He has led over 10 research projects funded by various organizations, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China and Tencent WeChat Rhino-Bird Project.
